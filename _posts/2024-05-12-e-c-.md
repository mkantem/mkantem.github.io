I know you will never be mine
But still I close my eyes 
And imagine how beautiful it would be
If we were together
In my imagination
The world seems to pause 
And everything feels right 
In my imagination
I picture us walking hand in hand
Sharing secrets under the stars
And laughing without a care in the world
As if our souls are united
panting the picture of a love so pure and tender
But reality tugs at my sleeve
Reminding me of the boundaries that separates us
And so I hold on to these dreams
Cherishing them
As precious whispers of what could have been 
Knowing that some stories are meant to remian untold
And some loves are menat to linger
In the silent corners of our hearts
But how impossible it may seem
In my imagination
I keep hoping that someday 
My dreams can come true
